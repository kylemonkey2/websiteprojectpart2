San Jose State Spartans!

The Spartans are a football team that represents SJSU. They have a bowl-record of 7-4. They intially started as a small team in the early 1890's, mostly competing against local schools. Throughout time, they've went on to compete in many national games. Today, you are able to try out and join the team as long as you pass the Academic Eligibility Requirements.

During the early 1970's to the 1990's, the Spartans hit a winning spree: winning 15 seasons, participating in 4 bowl games and had almost 50 players go to the NFL.

Around 19 years later, the slow but steady decline came for the Spartans when the team only had 1 winning season between 1993 and 2004 and inevitable budget cuts occurred.
